A, D: Rotate environment.
Up, Down, Left, Right: Move Ship.
Space: Fire.

A different take on a space shooter. You have to rotate your environment and move your ship to kill as many baddies as possible, but be accurate or you will get a low score. You can not hit enemies from the side, you have to user A and D to rotate the environment and hit them head on.

A perfect score is 100,000
